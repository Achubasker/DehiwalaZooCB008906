ACTIVITIES

Animals Feeding

The Zoo strives to provide the very best in animal care to the species in its charge.
"Carcass feeding"—feeding portions of meat or whole (dead) prey— is one of the ways keepers and nutritionists continually enrich animals' lives; to support an animal's physical, mental, and social health and to stimulate natural behaviors in the wild.
Time:
Morning- 8.00am-11.00am
Evening-4.00pm-6.00pm

Bird Watching

Bird watching is the viewing of birds in their natural habitat in the wild, or in their improvised habitats in urban areas, possibly even in your own back yard.
Bird watching involves identifying birds and understanding their behavior.
This fascinating hobby is becoming increasingly popular as a pastime enjoyed by young and old alike.
Time:
Morning-7.00am-11.00am
Evening- After 3.00pm

Elephant Show
